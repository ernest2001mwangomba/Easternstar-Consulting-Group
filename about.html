About Eastern Star Consulting Group

Eastern Star Consulting Group is a professional company offering a comprehensive range of services, including: Executive Search, Board and CEO Services, Outsourcing, Remuneration & Benefits Surveys, Family Business Advisory Services, Training, Performance Management, Leadership Development, Change Management, HR Policy, Organization Development, Talent Management, Labour Laws, and HR Advisory services. We have built a solid reputation for quality relationships, based on service delivery.

Established in 2021 in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, the company is planning to stretch its wings to the rest of Africa. People are at the heart of any successful enterprise, and acquiring/retaining top talent is mission critical to ensuring attainment of organizational objectives.

This is why our values are based on a firm commitment to find and attract the “right people for the right positions at the right time, for a great performance”.
